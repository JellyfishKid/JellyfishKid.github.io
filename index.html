INTRODUCTION
My name is Hannah Baez and I am a robotics engineering students at Worcester Polytechnic Institute in Worcester, Massachusetts. Most of the classes I take have a very technical focus such as autonomous navigation or the kinematics of serial manipulators, but every now and then I try and sneak in a class that allows me to express some creativity. The class that is the focus of this site, human computer interaction, is one of those classes. This class was perfect because I was able to mix my technical abilities with art. This was a specifically good opportunity for me because I am planning to work this summer at the Biosphere 2 in Arizona. There, I will be able to apply my engineering and new HCI skills to be able to develop tools for those living in the biosphere.
